Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Wars ("the App") handles information when you use it to generate and publish video content.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect or store any personal information on its own servers. All data remains on your local machine.

2. Third-Party Services

The App sends data to the following third-party services to function:

  • Anthropic — episode scripts and captions (text prompts)
  • ElevenLabs — voice synthesis (dialogue text)
  • Kling AI — video generation (scene descriptions)
  • Replicate — image generation (scene descriptions)
  • TikTok — video upload (video file and caption)

Each service's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data.

3. API Keys and Credentials

API keys are stored locally in a .env file on your machine and are never transmitted to any party other than the respective service they authenticate with.

4. TikTok Data

When you connect your TikTok account, the App receives an access token scoped to video.upload and user.info.basic. This token is stored locally and used only to upload videos to your account.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, please reach out via the app's GitHub repository.
